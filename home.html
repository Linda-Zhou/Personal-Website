About

I'm a data scientist - biostatistician in the Welch Center of Prevention, Epidemiology, and Clinical Research, Johns Hopkins University. I worked with 10+ clinicans and researchers on their data science projects, resolved their scientific questions of interest with the data in their hand, and successfully turned six projects into publications. I also managed the Johns Hopkins Hopspital clinical database for the devision of Nephrology, queried datasets for researcher with specific research needs. I have strong interest in data anlaysis, machine learning and building statistical models. I am passionate about making positive impacts with Data Science in real-world projects.
